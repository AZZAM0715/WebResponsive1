About The Organization

Giving is an institution, facilitator and mediator to help the orphans and dhu'afa get their rights to be able to enjoy a decent life and education where they are today, Giving is ready to synergize to all parties both institutionally and personally who want to contribute and real solutions in empowering orphans and dhu'afa and other general public.

The problems of orphans and Dhuafa during this increasingly uncertain economic crisis are really not the same as children who are under the assurance and protection of their parents, especially education and their future. Quality educational institutions are clearly out of reach for them, while education which is said to be "free" is of questionable quality, free and quality is still very rare and not comparable with their ever increasing numbers.

On January 2, 2019, to continue our program and provide a total solution for the orphans and the dhu'afa and the general public as a proof of all the contributions and mandates that have been entrusted to us. We established GVING, as a center of activity and miniature the system was open and happy.

GIVING is not an orphanage in general, because in it there are children who have not yet reached the mukalaf, but have been cut off from their parents and family, this orphanage is also a place to process the independence of children from the community who other general.

Let's support us to run this GIVING house to run well, give our support so that we can realize the vision and mission of GIVING to our children later.
